About

I am an Internal Medicine physician-in-training with a strong interest in aerospace medicine, hyperbaric and dive medicine, and medical education. My work bridges clinical practice, research, and innovation, with an emphasis on understanding and supporting human health in extreme and operational environments.

Throughout my training, I have been involved in multidisciplinary projects, ranging from spaceflight-related physiology and fluid shifts to operational challenges in remote and austere care. I enjoy collaborating across disciplines and working with teams that span medicine, engineering, and the life sciences.

Professional Interests

  • Internal medicine and critical care.
  • Aerospace medicine and space health.
  • Hyperbaric and dive medicine.
  • Medical education, curriculum design, and mentorship.
  • Data-driven approaches to clinical and operational questions.

Outside of Work

Outside of clinical and academic life, I enjoy scuba diving, spending time in coastal and mountain environments, and working on creative projects such as logo and patch design that reflect my interests in exploration and spaceflight.
